About Me

Hi there!

My name is Juan Villegas and I'm a Data Science major with a concentration in Math and a minor in Business Analytics. I have a passion for discovering insights through data and using that knowledge to drive meaningful change.

As a senior at Eastern Connecticut State University with an expected graduation date of May 2023, I have consistently earned a spot on the Dean's List every semester. Additionally, I have been actively participating in D3 Athletics as a member of my university's cross country and track team, where I have developed strong teamwork and leadership skills. Through my academic and athletic experiences, I have honed my ability to balance priorities and pursue excellence.

My technical skills include proficiency in Python, R, SQL, and Tableau. I also have experience with data preprocessing, explorartory data analysis, and buiding data science machine learning models. Additionally, I have strong communication and presentation skills, which enable me to effectively explain complex concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences.

An exciting project I am working on focuses on using data science techniques to make informed decisions when buying a house. The main objective is to gather data from Zillow's website using python's beautiful soup package and Selenium and store it in both SQL and NoSQL databases. The gathered data will then be analyzed using python data analysis packages like NumPy, Pandas, and Matlibplot. Finally, I will develop predictive and classification machine learning models to estimate the price of my "dream" house and identify the attributes to look for in a house within a given price range. If there is time left over, I will attempt to automate everything by using pipelines to perform web scraping, data exportation, data cleaning, EDA, and model building.

My current goal is to join a data-driven leadership development program, leveraging my skills to help businesses make data-driven decisions and enhance productivity. I am eager to learn and grow within a company that values professional development and provides opportunities for advancement. I am seeking a program where I can expand my skillset and contribute to the growth and success of both the organization and myself.
